Dalee — Support

Dalee is a daily word puzzle: one new five-letter word every day, six guesses, color-coded clues.

How to play

  • Green — right letter, right spot
  • Yellow — right letter, wrong spot
  • Gray — not in the word

Solve it before you run out of guesses, then come back tomorrow to keep your streak alive.
