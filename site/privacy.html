Dalee — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Dalee does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no logins, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party services.

Everything the game remembers — your guesses and your daily streak — is stored only on your device using local storage. It never leaves your phone and we never see it.

Because we collect nothing, there is nothing to request, export, or delete on our end. Deleting the app removes all local data.
